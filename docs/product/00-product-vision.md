# Restaurant Booking SaaS

## Product Vision

The platform is a SaaS application that automates restaurant reservations received through WhatsApp.

The objective is to eliminate manual reservation management while providing restaurants with a centralized dashboard to manage bookings, occupancy, availability, cancellations, modifications, reminders, and customer communication.

The system must be able to operate without AI during the MVP stage by relying on deterministic business rules and structured workflows.

The product must support at least 100 restaurants from the beginning and scale to thousands of restaurants without major architectural changes.

## Main Goals

* Automate reservation management.
* Reduce manual work.
* Improve customer experience.
* Increase reservation conversion rate.
* Reduce no-shows.
* Centralize reservation operations.

## Target Users

### Restaurant Customers

People who make reservations through WhatsApp.

### Restaurant Staff

* Owners
* Managers
* Receptionists

### SaaS Administrators

Internal operators responsible for platform management.

## Core Principle

95% of reservation requests must be processed automatically using business rules.

Artificial Intelligence should only be used as a fallback mechanism when messages cannot be interpreted reliably.
